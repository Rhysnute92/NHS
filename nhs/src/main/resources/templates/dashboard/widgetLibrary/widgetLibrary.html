Widget Library

This is a library of widgets that can be added or removed from your personal dashboard. The widgets provide convenient and quick access to your most used functions, like tracking your daily health actions.

Widgets Currently on Your Dashboard

To remove a widget from your dashboard, tap each widget icon to select them, then tap on the button saying "Remove Widget."

Available Widgets

To add a widget to your dashboard, tap the icon of the widget you want and then tap on the button "Add Widget."
